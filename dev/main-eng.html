Growth prospect of the token

YobiDoyobi’s revenue grows
2.5-3 times annually

From 2018 to 2021, the network's revenue increased from 106 million to 2.5 billion, that is, almost by 25 times. The average check for 3 years has grown by a third,and the number of customers has exceeded 2 million people.

The economy of the token is directly related to the activities of the company, which has been growing in all numbers for five years. Therefore, the token will grow like the company - not on rumors and hype, but on the growth of real business indicators.

The business is expanding: we are opening our branches in the CIS countries and an international company in Dubai

In the near future we are opening our branches in such countries as Romania, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Poland, Turkey, Azerbaijan, Hungary, Lithuania, Moldova, Belarus, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Romania, Slovenia. Plans for 2022-2023 are Dubai, Los Angeles, Istanbul and Portugal. By 2025 - Asia, Africa and Europe.

The expansion of the brand abroad will allow us to multiply all the company's indicators and make ourselves known to the whole world.

This will lead to the fact that foreign investors will become interested in the #YBC token, which will also positively affect the capitalization of the token in the future.

YobiDoyobi is part of the big Puzzle Food family

We are more than just sushi delivery. The international group of Puzzle Food companies also includes the “Tomato and Cheddar” pizzeria chain, the Big Russian Rolls sushi delivery chain and the Roman pizzeria chain “Puzzle Pizza”.

The combined growth of all brands in the portfolio will directly impact the success of the #YBC token, and the token itself will play a huge role in all of our projects, not just “YobiDoyobi”.

We burn 5% of tokens every year. The Less tokens there are, the higher their value is.

Every year, 5% of the tokens will be burnt until only 30% of the tokens of the total emission are left. Essentially, #YBC is a deflationary asset that decreases in supply over time and increases in value.

Thus, honest burning of tokens will stimulate its value and boost the value of the token for holders.
